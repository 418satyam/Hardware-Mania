Event Information

ROUND 1 — Identification Round (Solo)

  • This is an individual participation round.
  • Multiple unlabelled hardware components will be placed on the table.
  • Each participant gets 60 seconds to identify as many components as possible.

Qualification Criteria

  • Number of correct identifications
  • Difficulty level of the hardware identified

Qualified participants will move to Round 2 (Mystery Box Round).

ROUND 2 — Mystery Box Round

  • Multiple mystery boxes will be placed.
  • Each participant gets 15 seconds per box.
  • Only one chance is allowed per box.
  • Rules will be explained at the time of the event.

Winners are selected based on speed and accuracy.

General Instructions

  1. Participants must register at the entry of the IT Lab.
  2. Registered participants should settle inside the IT Lab.
  3. Participants will be called individually for Round 1.
  4. Only qualified participants must stay in the IT Lab after Round 1.
  5. For Round 2, participants will again be called individually to identify hardware from the mystery box.
