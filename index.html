Mode of Event

  • This is an offline individual event.
  • No team participation is allowed.

Rounds

  • There is only ONE round.
  • No second round, no re-attempts, and no extensions.

Time Limit

  • Each participant will be given exactly 1 minute.
  • The timer starts and stops strictly as announced by the judges.

Judging Criteria

  • Speed: Number of correct words typed within 1 minute.
  • Accuracy: Correct spelling, spacing, and punctuation.
  • Wrong Words:
    • Incorrect words reduce the final score.
    • Extra, missing, or misspelled words are counted as wrong.

Scoring Rule

  • Higher speed with fewer errors ranks higher.
  • In case of a tie, accuracy takes priority.

Disqualification Rules

  • Any form of cheating or external assistance leads to immediate disqualification.
  • Use of unauthorized devices is strictly prohibited.
  • Talking during the event is not allowed.
